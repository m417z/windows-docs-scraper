# RayDesc structure

Passed to the [**TraceRay**](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows/win32/direct3d12/traceray-function) function to define the origin, direction, and extents of the ray.

## Fields

**Origin**

The origin of the ray.

**TMin**

The minimum extent of the ray.

**Direction**

The direction of the ray.

**TMax**

The maximum extent of the ray.
