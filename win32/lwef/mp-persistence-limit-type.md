# MP\_PERSISTENCE\_LIMIT\_TYPE enumeration

## Constants

**MP\_PERSISTENCE\_NO\_LIMIT**

**MP\_PERSISTENCE\_DURATION**

Duration.

**MP\_PERSISTENCE\_VDM\_VERSION**

VDM version.

**MP\_PERSISTENCE\_TIMESTAMP**

Timestamp.

**MP\_PERSISTENCE\_FORCED**

Forced.
