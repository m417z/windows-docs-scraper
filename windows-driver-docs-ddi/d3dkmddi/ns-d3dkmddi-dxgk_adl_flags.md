## Description

The **DXGK_ADL_FLAGS** structure specifies the flag(s) that apply to an [address descriptor list (ADL)](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows-hardware/drivers/ddi/d3dkmddi/ns-d3dkmddi-dxgk_adl).

## Members

### `Contiguous`

Indicates that the pages in the ADL are contiguous.

### `Reserved`

Reserved for future use.

### `Value`

Another method to access the bits.
