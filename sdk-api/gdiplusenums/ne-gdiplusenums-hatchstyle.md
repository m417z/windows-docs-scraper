# HatchStyle enumeration

## Description

The **HatchStyle** enumeration specifies the hatch pattern used by a brush of type
[HatchBrush](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows/desktop/api/gdiplusbrush/nl-gdiplusbrush-hatchbrush). The hatch pattern consists of a solid background color and lines drawn over the background.

## Constants

### `HatchStyleHorizontal`

Specifies horizontal lines.

![Illustration of an ellipse filled with widely spaced horizontal lines over a background color](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows/win32/api/gdiplusenums/images/hatchstylehorizontal.png)

### `HatchStyleVertical`

Specifies vertical lines.

![Illustration of an ellipse filled with widely spaced vertical lines over a background color](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows/win32/api/gdiplusenums/images/hatchstylevertical.png)

### `HatchStyleForwardDiagonal`

Specifies diagonal lines that slant to the right from top points to bottom points. The lines are antialiased.

![Illustration of an ellipse filled with right-slanting lines over a background color.](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows/win32/api/gdiplusenums/images/hatchstyleforwarddiagonal.png)

### `HatchStyleBackwardDiagonal`

![Illustration of an ellipse filled with left-slanting lines over a background color.](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows/win32/api/gdiplusenums/images/hatchstylebackwarddiagonal.png)

### `HatchStyleCross`

![Illustration of an ellipse filled with a grid of horizontal and vertical lines over a background color](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows/win32/api/gdiplusenums/images/hatchstylecross.png)
